Control Panels

Plant Panel
  • Bottom
    • Used switches: 10 adjustable, 14 on/off (11 red, 4 black), 3 turnwitches
    • Reactor Loop - 4 on/off, 2 adjustable
      • 2 Steam separators
        • Steam separator 1..2 input on / off (red)
      • 4 Main pumps (adjustable)
        • Each pump supplies water to mostly one corner of the reactor
        • Main pump 1..4 on / off (red)
        • Increase / decrease main pump 1..4 power
    • Turbine Loop - 6 on / off, 4 adjustable
      • 2 Turbines (adjustable)
        • Increase / decrease steam throughput to turbine 1..2
        • Turbine 1..2 (steam input) on / off (black)
      • Steam condenser
      • 2 Water pumps (adjustable)
        • Increase / decrease water pump 1..2 speed
        • Turn water pump 1..2 on / off (red)
    • Cooling Loop -  2 on/ff, 2 adjustable
      • 2 River water pumps (adjustable)
        • Increase / decrease river water pump 1..2
        • Turn river water pump 1..2 on / off (red)
    • Emergency  - 3 red pushbuttons
      • Turn emergency cooling pump on / off (red)
      • Emergency venting of steam (into bubbler pool?  Or atmosphere?) (red)
      • Turn emergency diesel generators on / off (pushbutton, red)
    • Electricity - 3 turnswitches
      • Connect generator 1..2 to external power on / off
      • (Use external grid power for plant on / off ?)
  • Mid
    • Main pump x 4
      • Power 0..120 %
    • Steam separator x 2
      • Water temperature 0..400 Celsius
      • Steam pressure 0..10 MPa
    • Turbines x 2
      • Generated power  0..700 MW
    • River pump x 2
      • Power 0..120%
    • Cooling pump x 2
      • Power 0..120%
